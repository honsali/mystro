Rulers of Houses










Interpretations are being prepared.


















Ruler of I house in XII house


Toilsome youth, accusations and injury.

Ruler of I or Brothers with Cancer, Scorpio or Pisces with V signifies many siblings. Conversely, if they are with infertile images, it signifies few siblings or grief from such. Angular and with good stars upon the tenth makes them distinguished. With the VI or XII, it signifies enmity between the siblings or that the siblings are injured.





















Ruler of II house in I house


Great increase of livelihood, and good childhood.

If Saturn or Mars are with the II, V, VIII and XI, they harm livelihood and the accumulation of wealth. Loss is signified by the Ruler of the II, especially if inoperative or beheld by bad stars. Saturn causes loss from land, inheritance, old people, tombs, dead or worthless persons. Jupiter causes loss from superiors and public affairs. Mars causes loss from military affairs, wars, fires, lawsuits, fights or robbery. The Sun causes loss from the father, paternal relatives or on account of loans. Venus causes loss from affairs, graceful individuals or women. Mercury causes loss from education, lawsuits, inheritances, deception or separations. The Moon causes loss from the mother, or maternal relatives or suchlike persons.





















Ruler of III house in I house


Ruler of III well placed signifies increase of fortune and authority before death, but inoperative and beheld by bad stars, it signifies suffering around that time.





















Ruler of IV house in II house


Increase of wealth, sometimes through the parents or property.





















Ruler of V house in X house


Cheerful and social individuals and reputation after death.





















Ruler of VI house in VIII house


Ruler of VI when inoperative or beheld by bad stars signifies infirmities and accusations related to the image or the natural significations of the stars - Mars through fevers and lawsuits, Venus because of women, Mercury because of hearing, speech or throat impairments and similarly for the rest. If well placed, it signifies fortune before old age, or good relationship with subordinates.





















Ruler of VII house in VIII house


Increase or decrease of wealth through affairs.

Ruler of VII, Ruler of Affairs and Venus with changeable or double-bodied images signify many affairs. When they are under the beams, it signifies grief from affairs, or it makes them hidden.





















Ruler of VIII house in X house


Increase of property and prosperity.





















Ruler of IX house in II house


Ruler of IX when operative and beheld by good stars signifies good education and benefits from travel and foreigners. If inoperative and beheld by bad stars, it signifies misfortune in youth, dangers abroad or harm from foreign matters.






















Ruler of X house in I house


Successful and eminent individuals, especially around adulthood.

Ruler of X or Children with Cancer, Scorpio or Pisces with V signifies many children. Conversely, if they are with infertile images, it signifies few children or grief from such. Angular and with good stars upon the tenth makes them distinguished. With the VI or XII, it signifies enmity or that the children are injured. If the Lot and the Ruler are in harmony with the Hour-Marker and the Lot of Fortune, the children will be dear to their parents, they will be attractive, inherit the patrimony, but if they are unconfigured or diametrical, they will be quarrelsome, trouble-makers, injurious and will not succed to their patrimony. When Jupiter, Venus and Mercury are well configured, they signify brotherly affection and mutual respect amongst the children, but if they are unconfigured or diametrical, they will be quarrelsome and scheming.





















Ruler of XI house in I house


Increase of rank and gifts from the great.





















Ruler of XII house in XI house


Ruler of XII when inoperative or beheld by bad stars signifies suffering and accusations related to the image or the natural significations of the stars, or from servants, and sometimes it indicates uprisings of the multitudes and the victory of hidden enemies.























	






							



Main aspects - Aspects by Orbs










Aspects:


 























- Moon Square Saturn (6°03’, Separating)




Saturn square the Moon cause infirmities, the chilling of activities and anxiety, and it injures the mother, especially when Saturn is on the right of the waning Moon. If Saturn is with Libra, while the waxing Moon is with Cancer, the results are good, profitable and conductive of business, especially by day.






















- Mercury Square Jupiter (5°23’, Applying)




Jupiter square Mercury increase possessions and status, but they cause hatred from superiors, especially when declining.






















- Venus Conjunction Mars (5°07’, Separating)




Mars and Venus are inharmonious, productive of unsteady and weak mind, rivalry, crises, slanders, upsets, debts, men who have many friends, but who are blameworthy, shameless, passionate, fickle, malicious, plotters, murderers, spendthrifts, flitting from one occupation to another, eager for many things, who are wronged by women, especially by night and with Mars on the right. Mars and Venus setting under the beams signify secret adulteries, and if Mercury is present, but rising from the beams, it signifies discovery and public outcry.






















- Mars Opposition Saturn (4°18’, Applying)




Saturn opposition Mars are productive of poverty, grief from brothers, toilsome and despised occupations, captivity, dangers, infirmities and violent deaths.
